Who We Are!

A social community for anyone wanting to learn the skills of sewing for all purposes, whether it be as a hobby, finding a space for like minded creative or a simple introduction into the world of fashion design.

We hold classes for basic sewing skills and pattern cutting from our skilled Team Members We also provide open workspace sessions for all our members to have free creativity time, with off the clock assistance and guidance where required.

Meet our Team Sewers

I have met many individuals who would love to learnt he bas ic skills of sewing as a life social skill but dont know where to turn for a personable experience; so it led me to form this studio space for all who desire to learn. practice and create can do so, with others members close by and have the support they require."

Christine - Design Assistant and Freelance Pattern Cutter "I love knowing that we have a space in town for creatives of all levels to come togtehr. not only is this studio spacious, and the perfect environment to create but it is also a hub of community, a platform to network and build genuine connections. I came across the studio when I was searching for a space to pattern cut away from small single room and three years later i am so glad i dropped Susan that private message on Facebook!. I can not only work freely, but i get to help others to learn this great skill."

Carlos - In-House Machine Technician and Freelance Designer "Social Sewing became my Second Home three years ago, straight out of grad school- UAL ; and i have loved everyday since. not only do I get to share my knowledge and love for fashion design but i get to see others fall in love with it too.
